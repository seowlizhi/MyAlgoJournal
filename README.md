# MyAlgoJournal

MyAlgoJournal is a desktop application designed to help traders journal their trades and evaluate their performance both emotionally and strategically. By tracking emotional inputs and strategy types, traders can gain deeper insights into their trading behavior and improve their consistency.

## Features

### Trade Journaling
- Record and track your trades with detailed information
- Import live trades from MyAlgoManager (MT5 EA) for seamless journalling
- Evaluate performance based on emotional inputs and strategy types

### Performance Analytics
- **Calendar View**: Visualize your monthly progress and yearly net profits at a glance
- **Capital & Profit Graphs**: Track your current capital and profit trends over time
- **Emotional Analysis**: 
  - Pie charts showing entry emotions
  - Pie charts showing exit emotions
  - Track whether you're following your trading plan

### Goal Setting
- Set daily trading goals to prevent overtrading
- Limit daily trades, daily loss, and daily profit targets
- Stay disciplined with built-in trading constraints

## Purpose

MyAlgoJournal is part of the MyAlgoAssistant ecosystem, which aims to turn traders into consistent traders through utility tools and performance tracking. By combining trade data with emotional and strategic insights, traders can identify patterns, improve decision-making, and develop better trading habits.
